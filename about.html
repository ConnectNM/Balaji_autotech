About Balaji Autotech

Leading the Way in Metal Cutting Technologies

Balaji Autotech Pvt. Ltd. is a renowned company established in 2002 as a manufacturer, exporter, importer, service provider, and supplier of a wide range of CNC Plasma Cutting Machines, CNC Oxy-Fuel Profile Cutting Machines, Plasma & Oxy-Fuel Pipe Profile Cutting Machines, Machine Tools Retrofitments, Digital Readouts, Encoders, and Linear Scales.

As an exporter, we supply products to various parts of the world and are committed to total quality assurance of the products and services we offer. Our products are manufactured using advanced technology and are highly demanded by both large companies and small & medium enterprises for their modern designs, optimal performance, and longer functional life. We comply with international quality standards and develop products that undergo rigorous testing during pre-production and post-production stages.

Our proficient team of experts is a paramount factor in our success. With a large team of designers and engineers, we focus on product conceptualization and creation based on the latest technology. Our objective is to cater to the diverse requirements of our clients and ensure maximum satisfaction through sincere efforts. We also provide our clients with value-added after-sales and AMC services.

Since our inception, we have been centered on maintaining excellent quality standards. We firmly believe that quality is the decisive factor for success in the industry. We closely monitor every step of the production process to identify and eliminate any discrepancies that could lead to non-compliance with desired standards. Our products are subjected to testing based on various parameters such as durability, performance, ease of installation, and maintenance requirements to ensure they meet the highest standards.
